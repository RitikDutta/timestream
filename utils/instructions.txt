### Brief Instructions for Generating YouTube Timestamps

1. **Start at 0:00**: Begin with "0:00 - Introduction."
2. **Short Titles**: Keep titles concise, max 5 words.
3. **Key Points Only**: Include timestamps for major sections and transitions only.
4. **Consistent Format**: Use "[Time] - [Title]" format.
5. **Limit Quantity**: Aim for 5-10 important timestamps for a 10-15 minute video.
6. **Verify Accuracy**: Ensure timestamps align with actual video content.
7. **write in english**

Example:

```
0:00 - Introduction
1:30 - Overview
3:45 - Key Concept 1
6:00 - Topic 2
8:30 - Key Takeaways
10:00 - Conclusion
```
